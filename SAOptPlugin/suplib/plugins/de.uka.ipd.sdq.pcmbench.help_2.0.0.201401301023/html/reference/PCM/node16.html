Deployment

During deployment, the tested application is installed in its actual customer environment. The term deployment is also used to denote the process of putting components into component containers, but here the term refers to a broader task. Besides the installation, it might be necessary to adopt the resource environment at the customer's facilities or to instruct future users of the system with the new functionality. For the mapping of components to hardware resources, the deployment diagrams from the QoS analysis workflow can be used.
