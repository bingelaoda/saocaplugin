Visualisation

Figure 3.8: Short notation of assembly connectors and its meaning written using stereotypes

Figure 3.8 illustrates how the short notion of assembly connectors has to be interpreted correctly. In the short notion (at the top) assembly connector are drawn between a required and a provided interface. What is meant in the PCM is different. An assembly connector associates one provided and one required role. The latter associate one component and one interface.
